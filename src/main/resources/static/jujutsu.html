About Jujutsu Kaisen

Jujutsu Kaisen is a popular manga and anime series that follows the story of Yuji Itadori, a high school student who gets involved in the world of jujutsu sorcery. The series features intense battles, supernatural elements, and a rich storyline that captivates audiences around the world.

One of the main characters, Gojo Satoru, is known for his incredible strength and mysterious abilities. He plays a crucial role in the series as a mentor and protector of the protagonist and his friends.
